## Registration Process

To participate in the 340B program, a covered entity must complete OPAIS registration. The process involves:

1. **Identifying the covered entity type** that qualifies (DSH, FQHC, Ryan White, etc.)
2. **Designating an Authorizing Official (AO)** — a senior leader (CEO, CMO, CFO, etc.) authorized to bind the organization
3. **Designating a Primary Contact (PC)** — day-to-day compliance contact
4. **Registering all pharmacy locations**: in-house pharmacies, contract pharmacies, and child sites
5. **Completing the certification** that attests to compliance obligations

## Required OPAIS Data Elements

OPAIS requires covered entities to maintain current information for:

- Organization legal name and address
- Authorizing Official name, title, and contact information
- Primary Contact name and contact information
- Federal grant information (for grantee entities)
- DSH adjustment percentage (for DSH hospitals)
- Pharmacy locations (in-house, contract, child sites)

## Annual Re-Enrollment

Covered entities must **re-certify their participation annually** during HRSA's designated re-enrollment period (typically the last quarter of the calendar year).

During re-enrollment, the Authorizing Official must certify that:
- The entity remains eligible
- Program requirements are being met
- No diversion or duplicate discounts have been knowingly permitted

> **Consequence of non-renewal:** Failure to re-enroll results in **removal from the 340B program**. The covered entity cannot purchase drugs at 340B prices during the lapse.

## Update Obligations (10 Business Days)

Covered entities must update OPAIS **within 10 business days** of any of the following changes:

| Change | Must Update OPAIS |
|---|---|
| Adding a contract pharmacy | Yes, within 10 business days |
| Removing a contract pharmacy | Yes, within 10 business days |
| Adding a child site (outpatient department) | Yes, before 340B purchasing begins |
| Removing a child site | Yes, within 10 business days |
| Change in Authorizing Official | Yes, within 10 business days |
| Change in physical address | Yes, within 10 business days |

> **Key rule:** 340B purchasing for a new child site cannot begin until the site is actively registered in OPAIS.
